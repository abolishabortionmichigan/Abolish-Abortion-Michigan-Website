Purpose: reach out to allied abolitionist orgs listed on
`/partners` and ask them to link back to us. Reciprocal links between
same-topic sites are the highest-value SEO signal Google uses.

## Follow-up rhythm

- **Day 0**: send initial email
- **Day 7**: follow up ONCE if no reply — keep it short: *"Just
  wanted to make sure the note below reached you. No pressure if it's
  not a fit for your site — just wanted to close the loop."*
- **After that**: leave it. Silence is a no; there are 30+ orgs on the
  list and time is better spent moving to the next one.

## Track outcomes

Update `C:\Users\Dustina\Downloads\abolition_site_tracker.xlsx` under
the "Outreach Status" column when you send / hear back / see the link
go live. When a partner links back to us, note the URL where they linked
from — Google Search Console will pick up the backlink within a week
or two, and you can verify it landed under Search Console → Links →
External Links.
